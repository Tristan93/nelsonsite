Nelson Moore is intelligent, intuitive, and efficient. As a repairman, he has spent countless hours honing his craft, and has become reputable for repairing horns in the Middle Tennessee area. He has attended numerous repair workshops in order to augment his knowledge, and to network. With his level of hard work and dedication, Nelson is sure to be Nashville's next up and coming "go to" repairman. Nelson has earned a B.A. in Saxophone Performance from MTSU under the tutelage of distinguished professors Don Aliquo, and Paula Van Goes. Nelson is also available to teach private lessons to all ages and skill levels.
